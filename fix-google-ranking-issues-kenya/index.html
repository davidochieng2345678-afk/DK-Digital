Why Do Google Rankings Drop? 5 Common Causes

Rankings don’t drop randomly. There’s always a reason. Here are the most common issues we fix:

Algorithm Updates: Google releases thousands of updates yearly. Your site may have been negatively impacted.
Technical SEO Issues: Broken links, slow speed, crawl errors, or indexing blocks.
Bad Backlinks (Negative SEO): Toxic or spammy backlinks can trigger penalties.
Thin or Duplicate Content: Low‑quality content or duplicate product descriptions hurt rankings.
Mobile Usability Problems: Google uses mobile‑first indexing – if mobile is broken, rankings drop.
