Frequently Asked Questions — Traffic Growth SEO

How long does it take to see a traffic increase?
Typically, you’ll see initial improvements within 1–3 months (e.g., increased clicks and impressions). Significant traffic growth often takes 4–6 months depending on competition and your site’s starting authority. We provide monthly traffic reports to track progress.
What is the difference between basic SEO and traffic growth SEO?
Basic SEO focuses on rankings for a few keywords. Traffic growth SEO targets hundreds of long-tail queries, optimizes user engagement metrics, and builds topical clusters — resulting in sustainable visitor growth, not just ranking vanity.
Do you guarantee increased traffic?
While Google doesn't allow guarantees, we have a proven track record of increasing organic traffic for 90%+ of our clients. We offer a money‑back satisfaction guarantee if no measurable improvement after 3 months.
Can you help if my website is new?
Absolutely. We specialize in new website SEO — we focus on low‑competition long-tail keywords, content clusters, and foundational backlinks to kickstart traffic.
Do I need to give you full server access?
No. We work via Google Analytics, Search Console, and CMS user roles (WordPress, Shopify). Your site remains secure.
