  • 4. Direct outreach: Find blogs in your industry and pitch them directly – personalise each pitch

⚠️ What are the signs of a toxic backlink?

Toxic backlinks can hurt your rankings and even get you penalised. Here are the warning signs:

  • Spammy sites: Links from sites with low-quality content, excessive ads, or irrelevant topics
  • Link farms: Sites that exist solely to sell links – they often have "resources" or "links" pages
  • Foreign language sites: Links from sites in languages unrelated to your market
  • Low Domain Authority: DA 10 or less – these links pass little to no value
  • Unnatural anchor text: Over‑optimised, identical, or irrelevant anchor text
  • Sudden spikes: A sudden influx of 50+ links in a short period looks unnatural

Action step: Regularly audit your backlink profile using Ahrefs or SEMrush. Identify toxic links and submit a disavow file to Google Search Console. Protect your site – disavow toxic links.

📈 How many backlinks do I need to rank on page 1?

There's no magic number, but here's what typically works for Kenyan SMEs:

  • 15–30 quality backlinks: Competitive enough for most local keywords (DA 30+ sources)
  • 30–50 quality backlinks: Competitive for moderate keywords – positions 3–5 on page 1
  • 50+ quality backlinks: Dominant for competitive keywords – top 3 positions

Quality matters more than quantity: 20 links from DA 40+ sites > 100 links from DA 10 sites. Most Kenyan SMEs start with DA 8–12 and need 15–30 quality links to compete for page 1.

⚠️ Can backlinks hurt my SEO?

Yes – bad backlinks can seriously hurt your SEO. Google's Penguin algorithm penalises sites with unnatural link profiles. Here's what can hurt you:

  • Spammy, low-quality links: Links from link farms, spam sites, or irrelevant directories
  • Over‑optimised anchor text: Using the exact same keyword in every link
  • Unnatural link velocity: A sudden spike of 50+ links in a short period
  • Irrelevant links: Links from sites completely unrelated to your industry
  • Paid links: Buying backlinks from Fiverr or other cheap sources

Prevention: Focus on earning quality backlinks naturally. Avoid cheap link schemes. Regularly audit your backlink profile and disavow toxic links. Quality over quantity – always.
