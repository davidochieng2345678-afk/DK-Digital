🔎 [The 6 Components of Our Free Startup SEO Audit]

1. Complete SEO Health Check

70+ data points

We run your site through a comprehensive diagnostic that covers everything Google considers when ranking your pages. This isn't a superficial glance – we dig deep into every layer of your technical and content infrastructure.

On‑page SEO analysis: We review meta titles, descriptions, header tags (H1–H6), image alt text, and internal linking across your core pages. We identify pages that are thin, duplicate, or missing critical elements.
Technical SEO audit: We crawl your entire site (up to 500 pages) to find crawl errors, broken links (404s), redirect chains (301/302 loops), and orphan pages that Google can't find.
Page speed analysis: We measure Core Web Vitals (LCP, FID, CLS) on both desktop and mobile, identify render‑blocking resources, and provide specific recommendations to improve load times.
Mobile‑first indexing review: We test your site on multiple screen sizes, identify mobile usability issues (tap targets, font sizes, viewport settings), and ensure Google can properly render your content on mobile.
Schema markup audit: We check for structured data (FAQ, Product, LocalBusiness, Article, etc.) and identify opportunities to add rich snippets that improve click‑through rates.
XML sitemap & robots.txt review: We ensure your sitemap includes all important pages, is submitted to Google Search Console, and your robots.txt isn't blocking critical content.
Canonical tag audit: We check for duplicate content issues across your site and ensure canonical tags are correctly implemented to consolidate link equity.
HTTPS/SSL security check: We verify your SSL certificate is valid, check for mixed content warnings (HTTP resources on HTTPS pages), and ensure your site is fully secure.
Indexability analysis: We check how many of your pages are actually indexed in Google vs. how many you have – often startups have only 30% of their pages indexed due to technical issues.
Core Web Vitals dashboard: We provide a detailed breakdown of your scores across LCP, FID, and CLS – with clear recommendations to pass the Core Web Vitals assessment.

Long‑tail examples: We'll check issues like "how to fix LCP issues on a WordPress site", "why is my mobile Core Web Vitals failing", and "how to fix soft 404 errors on product pages."
