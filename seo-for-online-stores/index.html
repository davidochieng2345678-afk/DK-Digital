How long does e‑commerce SEO take to show results?
Typically 2–4 months depending on competition, number of products, and your store’s current authority. Some quick wins (like meta tag fixes) can show impact in weeks.
Do you work with Shopify stores?
Yes. We specialize in Shopify SEO optimization – from product pages to collections to technical settings (e.g., JSON‑LD, robots.txt, sitemap). We also work with WooCommerce, Magento, and BigCommerce.
Will SEO increase sales for my online store?
Absolutely. By targeting buyer‑intent keywords (e.g., “buy running shoes Nairobi”), we attract visitors who are ready to purchase. Our clients see average sales increases of 150%+ within 6 months.

Yes. Shopify often creates duplicate content for product tags and collections. We fix this using canonical tags, no‑index directives, and unique product descriptions.

Yes. We optimize image file names, alt text, and compress images for speed. Proper image SEO helps your products appear in Google Image search – a huge traffic source for e‑commerce.
