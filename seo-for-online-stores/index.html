E-commerce SEO is specialized for online stores. It focuses on product page optimization, category SEO, buyer-intent keywords, schema markup (Product schema), internal linking between products, and conversion optimization. It's designed to drive sales, not just traffic.

Think of it this way: Regular SEO gets people to your site. E-commerce SEO gets them to buy once they're there — and keeps them coming back.

We specialize in e-commerce SEO because online stores have unique challenges — duplicate content, faceted navigation, thin product descriptions, and slow site speed. We know how to fix them.

🔗 [How important are internal links for e-commerce SEO?]

Internal links are extremely important — they're one of the top 10 ranking factors for e-commerce stores. They help Google discover and index all your products, distribute authority across categories, and guide customers to related products.

Without strong internal links:

  • • Google can't find all your products (indexation issues)
  • • Authority doesn't flow to your best-selling items
  • • Customers can't navigate easily (higher bounce rates)
  • • Related products don't get discovered (lost sales)

Our internal linking strategy builds topic clusters and silo structures — a proven approach that boosts rankings for entire categories and product groups.

🧩 [What is structured data (schema) and why do I need it?]

Structured data (schema markup) is code you add to your product pages to help search engines understand your products better. It creates rich snippets — enhanced search results with product ratings, pricing, availability, and reviews.

Why you need it: Rich snippets stand out in search results and increase click-through rates by 30-40%. For product pages, the right schema (Product, Offer, Review, AggregateRating) can display:

  • • Price and availability — shows if the product is in stock
  • • Ratings and reviews — displays star ratings from customers
  • • Product images — shows product photos directly in search results
  • • Product descriptions — gives shoppers a preview of what you sell

We implement the right schema types for your store to maximize visibility and clicks. It's included in all our monthly packages.
