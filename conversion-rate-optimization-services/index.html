Frequently Asked Questions – Conversion Rate Optimization

What is Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)?
CRO is the process of improving your website to increase the percentage of visitors who complete desired actions – purchases, signups, form fills, or calls. It turns existing traffic into more customers without spending on ads.
Why is CRO important for my business?
Even high traffic doesn’t guarantee conversions. Most websites convert at 0.5‑2%. CRO can boost that to 3‑5% – tripling revenue from the same traffic. It’s the highest ROI marketing activity.
How long does CRO take to show results?
Initial improvements often appear within 2‑4 weeks (e.g., form optimizations, button changes). Significant lifts (A/B test winners) typically take 4‑8 weeks.
Do you implement changes after the audit?
Yes. We provide actionable recommendations and can implement the changes for you – from design tweaks to code changes. Or you can implement yourself using our detailed guide.

What’s the difference between SEO and CRO?
