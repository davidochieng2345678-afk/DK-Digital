🧠 Expert E‑commerce SEO Recommendation for Kenyan Store Owners

After optimizing over 100 online stores, we’ve identified three common mistakes that kill e‑commerce SEO:

  1. Duplicate product descriptions – using manufacturer descriptions across multiple products leads to thin content and duplicate content penalties. Always write unique descriptions.
  2. Ignoring site speed – every 1‑second delay reduces conversions by 7%. Most Shopify/WooCommerce stores have unnecessary apps or unoptimized images slowing them down.
  3. Poor internal linking – without a logical link structure from categories to products to blog posts, Google struggles to understand your site hierarchy.

Our recommendation: start with a free technical and product audit, then prioritize fixing duplicate content and site speed. This alone can double your organic traffic within 3 months.
