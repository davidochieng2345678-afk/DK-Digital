🧠 Expert Opinion – SEO for Online Stores & Startups in Kenya

According to our SEO specialists who have worked with 100+ e‑commerce sites and startups in Nairobi, Mombasa, and Kisumu:

  • For online stores: Duplicate product descriptions and slow speed are the #1 killers. Always write unique content and compress images.
  • For startups: Start with low‑competition long-tail keywords – you can’t outrank Amazon or Jumia for generic terms like “buy shoes”.
  • For both: Mobile optimization is non‑negotiable – over 70% of Kenyan searches happen on mobile. Ensure your store loads in under 2.5 seconds.
  • E‑E‑A-T matters: Google rewards sites that demonstrate Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness. Build reviews, case studies, and author bios.

Our recommendation: start with a free audit, fix critical technical and on‑page issues, then layer on content and link building. This approach delivers sustainable growth.
