⚡ Issue 1: Site Speed – The #1 Technical Factor

Most small business websites in Kenya load in 5‑8 seconds – Google's benchmark is 3 seconds

Speed is a direct ranking factor. Google prioritises fast‑loading sites – especially on mobile. Here's how to fix it:

❌ Common Speed Killers
  • Unoptimised images: Large files > 200KB
  • Too many plugins: Each plugin adds code
  • Heavy themes: Bloated website themes
  • No caching: Pages reload every time
  • External scripts: Slow third‑party code
✅ Speed Fixes
  • Compress images: Use WebP format, under 100KB

  • Use a fast theme: Lightweight, optimised theme

📈 LCP
> 4s

Fix: Defer non‑critical scripts, reduce JavaScript

> 0.25

🔍 Issue 4: Crawl Errors & Indexing – Can Google Find Your Site?

If Google can't crawl your site, you won't rank – no matter what

Googlebot needs to crawl and index your site. If there are errors, important pages won't appear in search results. Here's what to check:

❌ Common Crawl Issues
  • 404 errors: Broken pages and dead links
  • No sitemap: Google can't find all your pages
  • Robots.txt blocks: You're blocking Google from important pages
  • Redirect chains: Too many redirects confuse Google
  • Duplicate content: Google doesn't know which page to rank
✅ Crawl Fixes
  • Fix 404s: Redirect broken links to relevant pages
  • Submit sitemap: In Google Search Console
  • Check robots.txt: Ensure you're not blocking Google
  • Reduce redirects: Keep redirect chains to 1‑2 max
  • Canonical tags: Tell Google which page is the original

💡 Kenya example: A Nairobi small business had 50+ 404 errors from old product pages. We redirected them to similar services. Result: 30% more pages indexed, traffic increased by 40%. Google can now crawl and rank the entire site.

📄 Issue 5: Duplicate Content – The Hidden Penalty

Most small business sites have duplicate content – and don't even know it

❌ Common Duplicate Content Issues
  • Manufacturer descriptions: Copied from suppliers
  • Service pages: Similar descriptions across services
  • Blog excerpts: Same text on category and blog pages
  • URL parameters: Multiple URLs for the same content
  • No canonical tags: Google doesn't know which page is original
✅ Duplicate Content Fixes
  • Write unique content: Every page needs unique copy
  • Unique service descriptions: Each service page needs unique content
  • Canonical tags: Tell Google which page is the original
  • Noindex parameter pages: Prevent Google from indexing ?page=2

💡 Kenya example: A Nairobi small business had 10 service pages with identical descriptions. We rewrote each one uniquely. Result: service pages started ranking – 8 keywords hit page 1. Unique content = Google loves you.

  • Breadcrumb schema: Helps Google understand site structure
  • FAQ schema: For FAQ pages
