What Is Technical SEO?

Technical SEO involves optimizing your website so that search engines can crawl, index, and understand your content. Without it, your pages may never rank on Google.

Businesses use technical SEO services in Kenya to resolve these issues and boost online visibility.

---

1. Website Crawling & Indexing

Search engines use bots to crawl and index your pages. If your site isn’t crawlable, Google can’t rank it.

Check for:

  • Robots.txt errors
  • XML sitemap issues
  • Broken or redirecting links

Fix: Update your sitemap, fix broken links, and ensure no important pages are blocked.

---

2. Website Speed Optimization

Slow websites lose visitors and rankings. Speed is a confirmed ranking factor.

Improve performance by:

  • Compressing images without quality loss
  • Minifying CSS & JS
  • Using caching and CDN solutions

For a step-by-step speed optimization, see our On-Page SEO Checklist.

---

3. Mobile-Friendliness

Most users in Kenya browse on mobile. Google prioritizes mobile-friendly sites.

Make your site:

  • Responsive across devices
  • Fast-loading on mobile
  • Easy to navigate
---

4. HTTPS & Secure Website

Security matters for SEO. Google favors HTTPS websites.

Fix: Install SSL certificates and redirect HTTP pages to HTTPS.

---

5. URL Structure

Clean URLs improve user experience and SEO.

  • Keep URLs short
  • Include relevant keywords
  • Avoid unnecessary parameters

Example: /blog/technical-seo-explained-2026.html

---

6. Fix Broken Links

Broken links reduce crawlability and frustrate users.

Regularly check your website for 404 errors and fix or redirect them.

---

7. Structured Data (Schema)

Schema markup helps Google understand your content for rich results, which improves CTR and visibility.

  • Use BlogPosting schema for blogs
  • Use Product schema for e-commerce
  • Use LocalBusiness schema for local SEO
---

8. Internal Linking

Internal linking distributes SEO power, helps Google crawl your site, and guides visitors.

Always link to:

  • SEO services
  • case studies
  • contact section
---

9. Duplicate Content Issues

Duplicate content confuses Google and can reduce rankings.

Fix by using canonical tags and ensuring all content is unique.
