❌ 6 Common Mobile Optimization Mistakes to Avoid

Mistake 1: “Using a separate mobile site (m.)”
Google doesn't recommend separate mobile subdomains. Use responsive design instead – one store that works everywhere.
Mistake 2: “Mobile content is missing”
If content is on desktop but hidden on mobile, Google won't rank it. All important content must be visible on mobile.
Mistake 3: “Tiny tap targets”
Buttons smaller than 48px × 48px frustrate users and hurt conversions. Make them larger and well‑spaced.
Mistake 4: “Text too small to read”
If users need to zoom to read, you've failed mobile optimization. Use at least 16px font size for body text.
Mistake 5: “Slow mobile load times”
Over 50% of users abandon sites that take >3 seconds. Compress images, use caching, and minimize JavaScript.
Mistake 6: “No M‑Pesa integration”
Kenyan mobile shoppers expect M‑Pesa. Without it, you're losing sales. Integrate M‑Pesa into your Shopify checkout.
