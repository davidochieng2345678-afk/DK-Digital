✅ 7 Internal Linking Best Practices for Shopify

❌ "Click here to view shoes"

Don't just link to your homepage or collection pages. Link to specific product pages, blog posts, and category pages. "Deep linking" distributes authority throughout your store.

❌ Only linking to your homepage

4
Create a Hierarchical Structure

Link from top‑level pages down to sub‑pages. Homepage → Collections → Sub‑collections → Products. This creates a logical crawl path for Google.

Aim for 3‑5 relevant internal links per page. This ensures a good balance – enough to distribute authority and improve navigation, but not so many that it looks spammy.

Fix Broken Links Immediately

alt="Brown leather men's shoes from Kenya – handmade Oxford style"
