3. Step‑by‑Step: Run a Technical SEO Audit

3.1 Crawl Your Site with Screaming Frog (Free up to 500 URLs)

Download and install Screaming Frog (free version). Enter your website URL and click “Start”. Wait for the crawl to complete (usually 2‑5 minutes for a small business site).

Key reports to review:

  • “Response Codes” tab: Look for any 404 (broken internal links) or 5xx (server errors). Click on the 404 URLs to see which pages contain the broken links – fix them.
  • “Page Titles” tab: Identify missing, duplicate, too long (>60 chars), or too short (<30 chars) title tags. These need fixing in Step 4 (On‑Page SEO).
  • “Meta Descriptions” tab: Identify missing, duplicate, too long (>160 chars), or too short (<50 chars) meta descriptions.
  • “H1” tab: Find pages with missing H1 tags or multiple H1s. Each page should have exactly one H1.
  • “Internal” tab: Sort by “Inlinks” – pages with 0 internal links are orphans. Fix in Step 9 (Internal Linking).
  • “Redirect Chains” (Response Codes → Redirect Chains): Look for chains longer than 2 redirects. Aim for direct redirects (A → B, not A → B → C).
  • “Duplicate Content” → “Duplicate Page Titles” and “Duplicate Meta Descriptions”: Identify pages with identical meta data – fix uniqueness.

Actionable checklist from Screaming Frog:

  • ☐ Fix all 404 broken internal links (or add 301 redirects).
  • ☐ Fix any 5xx server errors (contact your hosting provider).
  • ☐ Write unique title tags (50‑60 characters) for every page.
  • ☐ Write unique meta descriptions (120‑158 characters) for every page.
  • ☐ Ensure every page has exactly one H1 heading.
  • ☐ Identify orphan pages and add internal links.
  • ☐ Simplify redirect chains to direct 301 redirects.

3.2 Check Google Search Console (GSC) for Indexing & Crawl Issues

Log into GSC. If you haven't verified your site, add your website property and verify ownership (HTML tag method is easiest).

Key reports to review:

  • “Pages” report: Shows which pages are indexed vs not indexed. Click on “Not indexed” – review the reasons (crawl anomaly, blocked by robots, 404, etc.). Submit important pages for re‑indexing.
