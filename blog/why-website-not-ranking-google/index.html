Why Your Website Is Not Ranking on Google (Fix SEO Guide 2026)

Launching a website is exciting, but seeing no traffic, leads, or sales can be frustrating. If your business in Kenya is invisible on Google, don’t worry — this is a common problem that’s fully fixable.

This guide will walk you through **why your website isn’t ranking** and **step-by-step actions** to get found online.

1. No SEO Strategy

Many websites fail because they don’t target specific keywords or audience intent. Generic terms like "digital marketing" won’t rank locally.

Fix: Build a keyword-focused SEO strategy using long-tail, local keywords:

  • “Best SEO services in Nairobi”
  • “Affordable digital marketing agency Kenya”
  • “Shopify SEO expert Nairobi”

Internal link: On-Page SEO Checklist

2. Google Hasn’t Indexed Your Site

If Google doesn’t know your pages exist, they won’t appear in search results.

  • Submit your website via Google Search Console
  • Request indexing for each page
  • Ensure your sitemap.xml is updated
  • Use internal links for page discovery

3. Weak or Thin Content

Google prioritizes content that is helpful, informative, and keyword-optimized.

Thin or irrelevant content fails to rank. Fix by:

  • Writing detailed guides relevant to your Kenyan audience
  • Targeting long-tail keywords like “local SEO strategies Nairobi”
  • Adding internal links to related posts

Internal links: Technical SEO Guide, Shopify SEO Guide

4. Poor Internal Linking

Internal links help Google crawl and understand your site. Without them, authority is diluted.

Fix: Link to:

  • Services
  • Case Studies
  • Contact Section

5. Technical SEO Issues

  • Slow website speed
  • Mobile responsiveness problems
  • Broken links (404 errors)
  • No HTTPS

Internal link: Website Traffic Growth SEO Guide

6. No Backlinks

Websites without backlinks lack authority. Google trusts pages that other sites link to.

Fix: Reach out for guest posts, industry mentions, or directory submissions.

7. Targeting Highly Competitive Keywords

Trying to rank for broad keywords like "SEO" is extremely hard for small businesses.

Focus on local, long-tail keywords like:

  • “Affordable SEO services Nairobi”
  • “Local SEO expert for online stores Kenya”
