The Rise of “Near Me” Searches in Kenya

In 2026, Google reports a surge in mobile searches containing “near me” keywords in Kenya. Users now expect immediate results for local services and products:

  • “Coffee shops near me Nairobi”
  • “Best plumber near me Mombasa”
  • “Yoga classes near me Kisumu”

Local searches are highly intent-driven — people searching “near me” are often ready to purchase or visit.

Step 1: Claim Your Google Business Profile

To show up in “Near Me” results, businesses need a fully optimized Google Business Profile (GBP):

  • Accurate NAP (Name, Address, Phone)
  • Business hours, website link, and photos
  • Category selection matching local searches
  • Regular updates and posts
