From Hidden to Highlighted: Getting Featured in Google’s Local Pack

For many Kenyan businesses, appearing in Google’s Local Pack feels like an impossible dream. This is the story of a small Nairobi boutique that went from invisible online to being highlighted in the Local Pack, attracting a flood of new customers.

Understanding Google Local Pack

The Google Local Pack displays the top 3 local businesses for a specific query, complete with map, contact info, reviews, and directions. It’s critical for any local business targeting “near me” searches.

Step 1: Optimize Your Google Business Profile

The boutique claimed its Google Business Profile (GBP) and optimized it:

  • Consistent Name, Address, Phone (NAP)
  • Category selection that matched customer searches
  • Business hours, photos, and description
  • Posts and updates showcasing promotions and events

Step 2: Gather and Manage Reviews

Customer reviews are critical for Local Pack rankings:

  • Encourage reviews through receipts and follow-ups
  • Respond professionally to all feedback
  • Highlight positive reviews on the website

Step 3: Build Local Citations

Consistency across directories signals trust to Google. The boutique added listings to:

  • Yelp Kenya
  • TripAdvisor
  • Yellow Pages Kenya
  • Local blogs and news sites

Step 4: Use Local Keywords Strategically

They focused on long-tail keywords such as:

  • “Boutique near me Nairobi”
  • “Affordable fashion store Kisumu”
  • “Best gift shop Mombasa”

Keywords were incorporated naturally into:

  • Page titles and meta descriptions
  • Header tags
  • Blog content and FAQs
  • Internal links to related posts

Step 5: Mobile Optimization

Most local searches happen on smartphones. The boutique’s website was optimized for:

  • Fast loading speeds
  • Responsive design
  • Easy navigation and click-to-call buttons

Step 6: Leverage Internal Linking

The boutique linked blog posts and landing pages to each other:

  • Journey of a Nairobi Business
  • Local Citations Case Study
  • Near Me Search Success

Internal linking helped establish topical authority and improved Local Pack rankings.

Results: Featured and Flourishing

Within 3 months:

  • Top 3 positions in Google Local Pack for multiple local keywords
  • Foot traffic increased by 200%
  • New customers discovered the boutique via mobile searches
  • Organic website traffic grew significantly
