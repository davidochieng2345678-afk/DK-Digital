3. No Traffic Strategy (Relying Only on Ads)

No visibility = no sales. Ads stop when you stop paying. Fix: Build organic traffic through SEO – it compounds over time.

4. No Blog Content (Missing Traffic Opportunities)

5. Poor User Experience (Slow Speed, Hard to Navigate)

Users leave quickly if your store takes >3 seconds to load or navigation is confusing. Fix: Compress images, remove unused apps, improve menu structure.

6. No Trust Signals (No Reviews, No Social Proof)

No reviews = no sales. Shoppers need proof that others bought and loved your products. Fix: Implement a review generation system and display ratings prominently.

7. No Conversion Optimization (Traffic Doesn’t Convert)

You might get traffic, but if your checkout is broken or CTAs are weak, sales won’t happen. Fix: Optimize CTAs, simplify checkout, add trust badges.
