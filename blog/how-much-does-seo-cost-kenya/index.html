Factors That Determine SEO Cost

1. Competition Level

Ranking for broad keywords like “SEO services in Nairobi” requires more effort than niche terms such as “affordable SEO for salons in Nairobi”.

2. Website Condition

Websites with slow speed, broken links, or poor structure need technical SEO optimization first. This includes:

  • Fixing broken links
  • Improving page speed
  • Optimizing mobile usability

3. Content Strategy

High-ranking websites regularly publish content targeting long-tail SEO keywords for small businesses in Kenya. Check our guide: Best SEO Services in Nairobi.

4. Backlinks & Authority

Google trusts websites that earn links from authoritative sources. High-quality backlinks significantly influence pricing because they take time and effort to acquire.
