Understanding Google Local Pack – What It Is and Why It Matters

The Google Local Pack displays the top 3 local businesses for a specific query, complete with map, contact info, reviews, and directions. It appears above organic search results and captures over 44% of clicks. For any local business targeting “near me” searches, ranking in the Local Pack is critical. This is especially true for near me searches Kenya like “boutique near me Nairobi” or “best coffee shop near Westlands”.

What is the Google Local Pack?
The Google Local Pack shows the top 3 local businesses for a search query, with map, contact info, reviews, and directions. It appears above organic results and captures 44% of clicks.

What is the most important Local Pack ranking factor?
Review quantity, recency, and sentiment – plus GBP completeness (categories, photos, posts) and citation consistency are the top three factors.

Do I need a website to rank in the Local Pack?

What’s the difference between Local Pack and organic rankings?
