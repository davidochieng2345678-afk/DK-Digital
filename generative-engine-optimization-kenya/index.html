14. How do Google AI Overviews differ from ChatGPT citations?

Google AI Overviews and ChatGPT citations serve different purposes in the AI ecosystem.

  • Google AI Overviews: These appear at the top of Google search results, providing AI-generated summaries of answers. They're directly integrated into Google's search interface and visible to all users. Google Maps optimization and local search are also influenced by these overviews.
  • ChatGPT Citations: These appear within ChatGPT conversations. Users actively ask questions and receive AI-generated responses that may cite your brand. ChatGPT has over 1.5 billion monthly active users.

Key differences:

  • Google AI Overviews are passive – users see them in search results.
  • ChatGPT citations are active – users are actively seeking information.
  • Both are valuable. Google AI Overviews provide visibility to search users; ChatGPT citations provide visibility to engaged, high-intent users.

16. What's the difference between GEO and AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)?

While GEO and AEO are related, they have different focuses:

  • GEO (Generative Engine Optimization): Focuses on being cited by generative AI systems like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Claude. The goal is to have your brand mentioned as a source in AI-generated responses.
  • AEO (Answer Engine Optimization): Focuses on appearing in direct answer features like Google featured snippets, voice search results, and "People Also Ask" boxes. The goal is to provide the direct answer that search engines display.

How they work together:

  • AEO gets your content featured in answer boxes
  • GEO gets your brand cited by AI systems
  • Together, they ensure your brand is visible across ALL search channels
