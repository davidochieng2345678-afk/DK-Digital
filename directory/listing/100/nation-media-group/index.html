About Nation Media Group

Nation Media Group (NMG) is the largest independent media house in East and Central Africa. Founded in 1959, NMG operates print, broadcast, and digital media platforms across Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, and Rwanda. With a mission to inform, educate, and entertain, NMG is a cornerstone of African journalism.
