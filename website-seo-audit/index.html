❓ What's the difference between technical and on-page SEO?
Technical SEO and on-page SEO are two sides of the same coin – both are essential for rankings.

🔧 Technical SEO
  • What it is: The behind‑the‑scenes elements that help Google crawl and index your site
  • Examples: Site speed, mobile optimization, XML sitemap, robots.txt, canonical tags, SSL/HTTPS
  • Who it affects: Search engines (Googlebot)
  • Impact: If technical SEO is broken, your content won't be found at all
📝 On‑Page SEO
  • What it is: The content and elements on your pages that tell Google what they're about
  • Examples: Title tags, meta descriptions, header tags, content quality, keyword placement, internal links, image alt text
  • Who it affects: Both search engines and users
  • Impact: If on‑page SEO is poor, your content won't rank even if technical SEO is perfect

The bottom line: You need both. At DK Digital SEO, our audit covers both technical and on‑page SEO – giving you a complete picture of your site's health.

❓ What are Core Web Vitals and do you check them?
Core Web Vitals are Google's official metrics for user experience. They measure:

  • LCP (Largest Contentful Paint): How long it takes for the main content to load – should be under 2.5 seconds
  • FID (First Input Delay): How quickly the page responds to user clicks – should be under 100 milliseconds
  • CLS (Cumulative Layout Shift): Whether elements move around unexpectedly – should be under 0.1

Do we check them? Yes – it's a standard part of our technical audit. We provide your current Core Web Vitals scores for both desktop and mobile – with specific recommendations to improve each metric.

Why they matter: Core Web Vitals are a confirmed ranking factor. Pages with "Good" scores outrank pages with "Poor" or "Needs Improvement" scores. At DK Digital SEO, we've helped clients improve their Core Web Vitals scores by 60%+.

🔍 What Our Website SEO Audit Covers (Deep Dive)

1. Technical SEO Audit

We examine your website’s backend to ensure search engines can properly crawl and index your pages. Issues we fix: site speed, mobile responsiveness, broken links, crawl errors, indexing blocks, duplicate content, and XML sitemap problems. A technical SEO audit is the foundation of all rankings.

2. On-Page SEO Analysis

We audit every important page for title tags, meta descriptions, heading structure (H1–H4), keyword placement, image alt text, and internal linking. Proper on-page SEO can double your click‑through rates from search results.

3. Keyword Research & Opportunities

We identify high-value local keywords in Nairobi (e.g., “best coffee shop in Westlands”, “affordable plumber near me”), buyer-intent terms, and long-tail phrases with low competition. You’ll get a list of 20+ keywords you can rank for quickly.

4. Content Optimization Audit

We evaluate your content quality, relevance, and alignment with search intent. Thin content, duplicate pages, and missing topic clusters are flagged. For content help, see our SEO content writing services.

5. Local SEO Audit

Local visibility is critical for Nairobi businesses. We analyze your Google Business Profile, local citations, NAP consistency, and reviews. For deeper local optimization, explore Local SEO Kenya and Google Business optimization.

6. UX & Conversion Analysis

We identify issues that prevent visitors from taking action: call-to-action placement, form friction, navigation problems, and page load delays. SEO without conversion is wasted traffic.
